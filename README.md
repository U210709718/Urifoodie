# Urifoodie
Urifoodie is a social media app for food lovers to share and discover meals and recipes. Users can create accounts to access features like:

Home Page: View and post food content.
Chat & Explore: Connect with friends and find new recipes.
Save & Favorite: Keep a digital recipe notebook.
Notifications & Profile: Stay updated and personalize your experience.
Calendar: Plan meals with a monthly list.
Our goal is to connect users worldwide, inspiring them to share and enjoy food experiences. We look forward to launching soon!
