# Urifoodie
Urifoodie is a social media app for food lovers to share and discover meals and recipes. Users can create accounts to access features like:
<ol>
<li>Home Page: View and post food content.</li>
<li>Chat & Explore: Connect with friends and find new recipes.</li>
<li>Save & Favorite: Keep a digital recipe notebook.</li>
<li>Notifications & Profile: Stay updated and personalize your experience.</li>
